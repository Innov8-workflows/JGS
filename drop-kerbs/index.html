Drop Kerbs in Derbyshire

Open Up Your Property — Approved Drop Kerb Specialists

A dropped kerb on the footway can completely transform the front of your property, allowing vehicles to move off the road and onto your driveway safely and legally. At JGS we are qualified, approved and insured to install dropped kerbs to the local authority specification — working hand in hand with Derbyshire County Council throughout the process. We manage the entire application and installation so you don't have to deal with the bureaucracy.

  • Full council liaison and application support
  • Installation to local authority specifications
  • Qualified to Street Works standard (SWQ No. 00808775)
  • Approved by Derbyshire County Council
  • Fully insured and compliant installation
  • Coordinated with new driveway installation if required
