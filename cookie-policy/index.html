What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to website owners.

This policy explains what cookies we use on jgslimited.co.uk, why we use them, and how you can control them.

How We Use Cookies

JGS Construction Services Limited uses a small number of cookies to ensure our website functions correctly and to understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it. We do not use cookies for advertising purposes and we do not sell your data to third parties.

Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Type Purpose Duration
jgs_cookie_consent Essential Stores your cookie consent preference so we don't ask again on every visit. 1 year
_ga Analytics Google Analytics — used to distinguish users and track how visitors use the site. No personally identifiable information is stored. 2 years
_ga_* Analytics Google Analytics — used to persist session state. 2 years
_gid Analytics Google Analytics — used to distinguish users over a shorter period. 24 hours

Essential cookies are required for the website to function and cannot be switched off. Analytics cookies are only set if you accept cookies.

Your Choices

When you first visit our website, you will be shown a cookie banner giving you the choice to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can change your preference at any time by clicking the button below.

Clicking this will reload the cookie consent banner so you can update your choice.

Managing Cookies in Your Browser

You can also control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that if you disable cookies entirely, some parts of our website may not function correctly.

  • Google Chrome — manage cookies
  • Mozilla Firefox — manage cookies
  • Microsoft Edge — manage cookies
  • Apple Safari — manage cookies

Third Party Services

We use the following third-party services to help operate this website. These services only process visitor data after you have given consent via our cookie banner.

  • Google Analytics — helps us understand how visitors use our website. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect information about your visit in an anonymised form, including the number of visitors, where visitors have come from, and the pages they visited. For more information, visit policies.google.com/privacy.
  • Innov8 Workflows analytics — Innov8 Workflows Ltd (innov8workflows.co.uk) processes anonymised visit data, click events (phone calls, WhatsApp messages, form submissions) and page views on our behalf so we can understand how customers interact with our site and improve our service.
  • Web3Forms — when you submit our contact form, your message is delivered to us via Web3Forms. They only process your form data (name, phone, email, address, message) for the purpose of delivering your enquiry to us.

We do not use Facebook Pixel, advertising networks, or any other third-party tracking beyond the services listed above.

You can withdraw consent at any time by clearing your cookies for this site, after which all consent-gated tracking will stop until you opt in again.
